      About / Mission

      This site is supposed to sound like me.

      I wanted it to feel real and personal, not like a template trying too hard.

      I like Tony Stark and JARVIS because the tech feels smart, clean, and visible. That affects how I think about software, especially dashboards, AI tools, and anything that feels interactive.

      I am a 9th grade student in Texas, and outside school I spend time on soccer, gaming, and Marvel stuff. The classes I care about most are science and math, and a lot of that thinking ends up in the way I build projects.

      I am putting my work here because I want a real record of how I started. If I look at this again ten years from now, I want it to actually sound like me and show what I was building at the beginning. The long goal is becoming an OpenAI full-stack engineer.
